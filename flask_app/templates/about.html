About Us

Main Idea

As college students, we know how overwhelming it can be to be in a completely new town. We wanted to create a website that helps people plan out an adventure, regardless of their interests or what they're feeling for the day.
Not only is our goal to help people become acquainted with a new environment, but we also wanted to create a site that promotes small businesses.

How it Works

Users can input their interests to help curate their perfect day in their town, specifically targeting small businesses and underrated spots!
